Our Camping Trip to Watkins Glen, NY

(the first of many)

Watkins Glen is located at the south end of Seneca Lake in New York.

We highly recommend it! Here's why:

  • there is a long waterfall trail within walking distance from camp sites
  • lots of local farmer's markets within driving distance
  • clean bathrooms, showers, and camp sites
  • mild weather

What we brought:

  • a thrifted tent in like-new condition
  • a foldable chair and a hammock (also thrifted)
  • sleeping mats, blankets, pillows
  • flashlights, lanterns
  • first aid kit and toiletries
  • food: fruit, vegetables, cheese, bread, trail mix, beans
  • cooking supplies, including a cast irion pan
